Surgery
OrthoFlow
A very handy guide for common fracture management. I have found this app incredibly useful during my time in Trauma & Orthopedics.
Orthobullets
This app gives clear and coherent information (it's in bulletpoints!) surrounding orthopaedic conditions.
Teach Me Anatomy
Great app for a quick anatomy reminder or for exam revision with its sections detailing core regions of clinical signifiance.
Teach Me Surgery
Similar to Teach Me Anatomy however with a greater focus on key surgical anatomy, conditions and procedures. I found this very useful during my General Surgery rotation.
Accident & Emergency
Toxbase
This app gives you rapid guidelines for managing the most common overdoses.
iResus
Easy to access guidance from the Resus Council including adult and paediatric and neonatal guidelines.
Assorted Clinical Apps
BNF
This app is a fundamental when you want to look up drug doses, contraindications or clinical guidance. I think it's much better than buying a physical copy every 6 months...
BMJ Best Practice
Simple app, brilliant for an overview of most medical conditions including history, investigation and management.
MDCalc
This app speeds up looking up scoring systems, allowing you to work more efficiently.
Stethocope
Late night ward calls? Not sure what to do? Breathe... and read this.
Induction Switch App
If you need a number, here's your directory! This app has commonly used phone numbers and is regularly updated by the user-base allowing you to skip out switchboard!
Revision resources
BMJ On Exam
Good for exam revision.
Osmosis
Another resource for exam revision.
Passtest
A regularly updated question bank, great for all sorts of examinations.
GeekyMedics
Great to cover examinations, commonly used when revising for medical school OSCEs.

Wellbeing and Support

Support

This carefully curated list includes a range of tools and information to address various aspects of well-being, encompassing physical, mental, and emotional health. If you require specific support see the advice on how to ask for that below via the STEP form.

The BlueLightCard is a service for healthcare professionals and emergency service workers where you get access to discounts for a massive variety of products and services. Some of the services e.g. Gym classes listed below will have substantial discounts if purchased through them.

Physical Wellness

  1. Going for a run, a swim or weight training exercises can help your body produce endorphins and dopamine. It's hard to get into a habit after work, so it might help to look for gyms with classes so you can sign up for sessions that work alongside your shifts.
  2. Unsure how much exercise is recommended? See the NHS Website: Exercise page and select your age group.
  3. Home workouts were at a peak during the COVID-19 Pandemic which led to a huge increase in online exercise classes. You can find them on YouTube and once again the NHS Website

Mental Health Support

  1. Find local mental health charities through Hub of Hope
    2. and Mental Health Foundation.
  2. For further information you can look at the NHS Website: Mental Health.
  3. Remember to book annual leave in advance, this way you can have something extra to look forward to.
  4. Take time for yourself. There may be times when you feel like you're not getting that much "you" time. For some, it can help to set time aside to do things that you enjoy, for example, hobbies, attending groups or even just watching a good TV show in the comfort of your home.

STEP form

  1. The STEP form ( Supporting Trainees Entering Practice) is a way in which suitable adjustments and support plans can be put in place for your clinical practice. The form should be completed PRIOR to starting work and sent to the Foundation School in which you will start working. For more guidance, including a template of the form Click here.

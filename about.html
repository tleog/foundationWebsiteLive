About me

Hello, my name is Tom.

I am a Doctor working in the UK with a passion for medical education and sharing knowledge.

In the week before commencing as a junior doctor, I had a brief discussion with someone who explained the details of the payslip we would receive at the end of the month. This information was entirely new, and it felt somewhat last-minute. It prompted me to consider what other information I might have learned on the job that could have been shared earlier.

Because of this, I've created this platform as a project to assist clinicians and medical students in understanding topics that may not be clear before beginning clinical work.

If you found this resource helpful and want to support me further, consider leaving a tip at Ko-fi.com

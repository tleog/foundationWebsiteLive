Who are we?

Hello, my name is Tom.

I am a Doctor working in the UK with a passion for medical education. After completing foundation training I decided to spend some years working as a teaching and research fellow. Within this role I have been involved with teaching all years of medical students from a UK-based medical school in and not limited to clinical, tutorial and lecture based teaching sessions covering a variety of topics. This role has been one of continuous learning and growth and has fuelled my enthusiasm for medical education. I firmly believe that knowledge should be passed on to the next generation of healthcare professionals, be it core medical knowledge or vital career and life advice when starting in such a field.

In the week prior to starting as a junior doctor I had one small talk from a doctor breaking down the pay slip we would receive at the end of that same month. This information was brand new and it almost felt a bit last minute. This made me think, what other information did I learn on the job, or last minute prior to starting that could have been delivered prior?

As a result I have developed this platform as a project with the aim to help clinicians and medical students with topics that aren't always clear prior to starting or even during your job as a doctor.

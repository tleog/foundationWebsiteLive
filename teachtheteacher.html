Train the Trainer: The art of pedadogy

In order to be able to teach medical students, doctors and other healthcare professionals one must study the art of pedagogy. Below we have listed core concepts in teaching medical students.

  • Clinical Expertise: In-depth knowledge of medical science and clinical practices is fundamental to guide students effectively.
  • Communication Skills: Clear and effective communication is crucial for explaining complex medical concepts and building rapport with students.
